COVID-19 Mythbusters

  • Myth 01

    Everyone should wear a mask

    No, everyone does not need to wear a mask. Wear a mask if you:- have symptoms, such as coughing or sneezing.

  • Myth 02

    COVID-19 is transmitted by eating non-veg.

    There is currently NO evidence for COVID-19 transmission due to eating eggs, chicken and meat.

  • Myth 03

    COVID-19 can be transmitted from pets.

    There is NO evidence that companion animals/pets such as dogs or cats are spreading disease to humans

  • Myth 04

    COVID-19 cant survive in high temperature.

    COVID-19 can be transmitted in ALL areas including areas with hot and humid climates.Regardless of climate.

  • Myth 05

    COVID-19 can be transmitted through mosquito bites.

    Scientists have confirmed that Covid-19 pandemic cannot be transmitted to people by mosquitoes

  • Myth 06

    Antibiotics are effective in preventing and treating the new coronavirus

    Antibiotics work only against bacteria, not viruses.
